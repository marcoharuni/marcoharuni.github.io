About

MH
San Francisco, CA

Marco Haruni is an AI researcher focused on building intelligent systems that can learn, reason, and generalize across complex domains.

His work sits at the intersection of deep learning, natural language processing, and scalable system design. With a background spanning both academic research and industry engineering, Marco brings a unique perspective to the challenge of making AI both capable and reliable.

Currently exploring approaches to improve the reliability and interpretability of large language models, with a focus on making AI systems more aligned with human values and intentions.

I'm particularly interested in bridging the gap between cutting-edge AI research and practical applications that can make a real difference in people's lives.
