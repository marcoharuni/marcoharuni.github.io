some thoughts

I’m deeply interested in multimodal systems — models that combine vision, speech, text, and robotics — especially when they reflect our cultures, preserve human values, and actively protect humanity.

I care strongly about open source and open research. I enjoy teaching and learning about AI systems architecture and robotics as practical, understandable systems — not abstractions.

I’m especially curious about household robots: systems that help with everyday chores, reason with little data, adapt to real environments, and grow alongside humans.

I don’t value most advice. Much of it reflects other people’s eras, experiences, and biases. What matters is action — take one belief you think could change lives and act on it. Through doing, you’ll learn a lot, and understand a little about life.
