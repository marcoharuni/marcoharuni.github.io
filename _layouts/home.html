some thoughts

I’m deeply interested in multimodal systems models that combine vision, speech, text, and robotics, especially when they reflect our cultures, preserve human values, and protect humanity.

I care strongly about open source and open research. I enjoy teaching and learning AI systems architecture and robotics as practical systems.

I’m curious about household robots systems that help with everyday chores, reason with little data, and adapt to real environments.

I don’t value most advice. Much of it reflects other people’s eras and biases. What matters is action — take one belief and act on it. Through doing, you’ll learn a lot, and understand a little about life.
